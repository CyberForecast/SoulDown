What is SoulDown?

SoulDown is a powerful desktop application written in Python that lets you effortlessly download entire albums or individual songs from YouTube. Whether you’re looking to archive your favorite music or simply download a single track, SoulDown offers you the flexibility to do so in a variety of audio qualities. Plus, with automatic album organization and custom download queues, your music collection will be perfectly structured and easy to manage.

Key Features

Complete Album Downloads

Simply enter the URL of a YouTube playlist or album and download all the tracks in one go. Perfect for music lovers.

Individual Song Downloads

Select specific songs from any album or playlist and download them directly to your computer. Full control over your collection.

High-Quality Audio

Choose from multiple audio quality options (96kbps, 128kbps, 320kbps) to get the best sound for your needs.

Automatic Organization

SoulDown automatically organizes your downloads by artist and album, so you don’t have to worry about messy files.

Album Art Downloads

Download album covers and assign them to each song, so your music collection looks as good as it sounds.

Queue Management

Add multiple albums to a queue, and download them in sequence. Monitor download progress for both individual and total queue.

Why Choose SoulDown?

SoulDown is designed to make downloading YouTube music easier and more efficient. With an intuitive interface, advanced features, and complete customization options, it’s the perfect tool for both casual listeners and dedicated music collectors. Plus, it’s completely free and open source under the Apache 2.0 license!
