What is SoulDown?

SoulDown is a powerful desktop application written in Python that lets you effortlessly download entire albums or individual songs from YouTube. Whether you’re looking to archive your favorite music or simply download a single track, SoulDown offers you the flexibility to do so in a variety of audio qualities. Plus, with automatic album organization and custom download queues, your music collection will be perfectly structured and easy to manage.

Complete Album Downloads

Download entire albums or playlists with a single click, ideal for music collectors.

Individual Song Downloads

Choose specific songs from any album or playlist to download them individually.

High-Quality Audio

Get your music in multiple quality options: 96kbps, 128kbps, and 320kbps.

Automatic Organization

Automatically organizes your downloads by artist and album for easy access.

Album Art

Download album covers and assign them to your music for a complete experience.

Queue Management

Create a download queue to process multiple albums and tracks in sequence.

Open source

All code is released under the Apache-2.0 license.

Free and easy
